# Betta Fish

## Overview
Betta fish, also known as Siamese fighting fish, are popular for their vibrant colors and elaborate fin displays. They are native to Southeast Asia and are known for their aggressive behavior towards other males.

## Characteristics
- **Scientific Name:** Betta splendens
- **Family:** Osphronemidae
- **Size:** 2.5-3 inches
- **Lifespan:** 2-4 years

## Habitat
Betta fish are found in shallow waters such as rice paddies, ponds, and slow-moving streams. They prefer warm, stagnant water with plenty of vegetation.

## Behavior
Betta fish are territorial and aggressive, especially males. They build bubble nests for breeding and can breathe atmospheric air thanks to their labyrinth organ.
