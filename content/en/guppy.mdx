# Guppy

## Overview
Guppies are one of the most popular and easy-to-care-for freshwater aquarium fish, known for their vibrant colors and lively behavior. They are native to northeast South America.

## Characteristics
- **Scientific Name:** Poecilia reticulata
- **Family:** Poeciliidae
- **Size:** 1.5-2 inches
- **Lifespan:** 2-3 years

## Habitat
Guppies thrive in a variety of freshwater habitats including streams, rivers, and ponds. They prefer slightly warm waters.

## Behavior
Guppies are peaceful and social fish, often seen swimming in schools. They are livebearers, meaning they give birth to free-swimming young rather than laying eggs.
